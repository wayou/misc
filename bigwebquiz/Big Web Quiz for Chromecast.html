Help

Why are these questions so funky?

All of the Big Web Quiz questions were generated by computers! Using data from Knowledge Graph and popular search queries, we were able to generate thousands of fun and wacky questions to test your brain.

What is Google Knowledge Graph?

Google Knowledge Graph is a knowledge base that helps to enhance search results with semantic-search information gathered from a wide variety of sources. What that means is instead of having to click through to a site, we are able to give you answers, right in the search window!

How are these questions generated?

First, we looked through all of the possible information we could get from Knowledge Graph. Then, a team of Knowledge Graph engineers wrote scripts to build questions. We made sure the questions were relevant by including trending topics!

Do I need Chromecast to play Big Web Quiz?

Yes. Big Web Quiz is made for Chromecast so you’ll need to have a Chromecast to play. Get started by downloading the app for iOS and Android.

What if I can’t connect to the Chromecast?

First, make sure that your device is on the same wifi network as your Chromecast. If you still can’t connect, try these troubleshooting tips here.

How many questions are in every game?

There are ten questions in every game.

How are the scores tallied?

Good question! There are three main ways that scores are tallied based on what question type you’re playing.

Multiple Choice: Scoring for multiple choice questions is based on time. If you answer with 10 seconds left on the clock, you get 100 points. If you answer with nine seconds remaining on the clock, you get 90 points. If you answer with two seconds left on the clock, you get 20 points. And so on! If you answer incorrectly at any time, you get zero points. You can change your answer until time has expired or until everyone in the game has submitted an answer. When changing your answer, you will be scored on the time of your last change. This includes maps questions and image questions as well!

Slider Questions: Scoring for slider questions is based on how close you are to the correct answer. If you are within 25% of the correct answer, you get 100 points. If you are within 50% of the correct answer you get 50 points. If you are within 75% of the correct answer, you get 25%. If you are further away than that, you get zero.

Ordering Questions: Scoring for ordering questions is simple. If you get it right, 100 points. If you get it wrong, zero points!

How do I get a Gold Chain?

Gold chains are awarded to the winners of games. There are a lot of cool prizes that your avatar can get, and the more players you play with, the more prizes you can unlock!

Does the speed of my answer affect my score?

On multiple choice questions, the faster you answer, the more points you get!

The screen is frozen. What now?

Oh no! First, make sure your Chromecast didn’t lose connection to the internet. Follow the troubleshooting tips here. If that still doesn’t work, you might need to unplug your Chromecast and plug it back in!

What kind of device can I use with Big Web Quiz?

Big Web Quiz works with most Android and iOS devices.

Can I play with someone that’s not in the same room?

No, Big Web Quiz is made to play with your friends, in the same room!

Why is Big Web Quiz obsessed with the size of dogs?

Good question! Knowledge graph knows the heights of many different entities, like mountains, celebrities and dogs! Our question generating computers were able to use these heights to create crazy questions.

Sometimes entities have multiple heights. Like a dog has a different height when standing on all fours than it does when standing just on its hind legs. In these cases, we used an average of the two heights.
